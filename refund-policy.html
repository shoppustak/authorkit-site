Refund Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

At AuthorKit, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds on our premium WordPress plugins and services.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all premium plugin purchases. If you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you may request a full refund within 30 days of your purchase date.

Eligibility for Refunds

To be eligible for a refund:

  • The refund request must be made within 30 days of the original purchase date
  • You must provide a valid reason for the refund request
  • The license key must not have been shared or distributed to unauthorized parties
  • You must have made a reasonable attempt to resolve any technical issues with our support team

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Free plugins (as they are provided at no cost)
  • Purchases made more than 30 days ago
  • License renewals (after the renewal period has begun)
  • Custom development work or consulting services (once delivered)
  • Accounts suspended or terminated for violation of our Terms of Use

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team at support@authorkit.pro
  2. Include your order number and the email address used for purchase
  3. Provide a brief explanation for the refund request
  4. Our team will review your request within 2-3 business days

Refund Processing

Once your refund request is approved:

  • Your license key will be deactivated immediately
  • The refund will be processed through our payment processor, Lemon Squeezy
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for purchase
  • Please allow 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been processed

Subscription Cancellations

For annual license subscriptions, you may cancel at any time. However, refunds for subscription cancellations are only available within the first 30 days of the initial purchase or renewal. If you cancel after 30 days:

  • Your subscription will not auto-renew at the end of the current billing period
  • You will retain access to updates and support until the end of your paid period
  • No partial refunds will be issued for the unused portion of your subscription

Technical Issues

We are committed to helping you resolve any technical issues you may encounter. Before requesting a refund due to technical difficulties, please:

  • Contact our support team at support@authorkit.pro
  • Provide detailed information about the issue, including error messages and screenshots
  • Allow our team reasonable time to investigate and resolve the issue

Many technical issues can be quickly resolved with proper support. If we are unable to resolve a legitimate technical issue that prevents you from using the plugin, you will be eligible for a full refund regardless of the 30-day window.

Chargebacks

We ask that you contact us directly before initiating a chargeback with your credit card company. Chargebacks should be a last resort after exhausting all other options. If a chargeback is initiated:

  • Your license will be immediately deactivated
  • Your account may be permanently banned from our services
  • We reserve the right to pursue collection of any outstanding amounts

Fair Use Policy

We reserve the right to deny refund requests that appear to violate our fair use policy, including but not limited to:

  • Repeated purchase and refund requests
  • Refund requests after extensive use of the product
  • Requests that appear to be fraudulent or abusive
  • Violations of our Terms of Use

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. Your continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.
