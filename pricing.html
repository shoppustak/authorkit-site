Common questions

What is founding member pricing?

The first 150 customers get a permanently discounted rate — $49/year instead of $79/year (annual), or $149 instead of $249 (lifetime). Your rate never increases as long as you keep your license active.

What is lifetime pricing — is it really forever?

Yes. Pay once and own it. No renewal required. You receive all updates and new modules as long as the product exists. It's how Vellum and Atticus work — you buy it, you keep it.

What happens when my annual license expires?

Your plugin continues to work. You lose access to updates, new modules, and priority support until you renew. Your books, data, and settings are never affected.

What's included in Pro?

Pro includes unlimited books and reviews, advanced book display templates, enhanced SEO features, priority support, and all future features we build. Your Pro license is a growing bundle — new tools are added regularly at no extra charge.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes — 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans, no questions asked. View refund policy.

Why not just use Shopify or WooCommerce for my author website?

Shopify costs $39–$105/month before any apps — that's $468–$1,260/year. WooCommerce is free but requires significant setup, hosting, and plugin investment. If you're already on WordPress, AuthorKit adds author-specific features to your existing site at a fraction of the cost.
