Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AuthorKit

Is AuthorKit free to use?

Yes! AuthorKit is available for free on WordPress.org with limits on books (10) and reviews (3 per book). All books sync to the free Bookshelf catalog automatically. Premium plans with unlimited usage and advanced features are available for authors who need more power.

Do I need to know how to code to use AuthorKit?

Not at all. AuthorKit is designed for authors, not developers. Install from your WordPress dashboard in minutes — no coding required.

Will AuthorKit work with my existing WordPress theme?

AuthorKit is 100% theme compatible and works with popular themes like Kadence, Astra, Divi, GeneratePress, and virtually any other WordPress theme.

Can I use AuthorKit if I have books on Amazon, Kindle Unlimited, or other retailers?

Absolutely. AuthorKit lets you link to all major retailers including Amazon, Barnes & Noble, Kobo, Apple Books, and any direct sales platform.

Does AuthorKit slow down my website?

The opposite. AuthorKit is built with vanilla PHP and JavaScript — no heavy dependencies — making it one of the lightest author plugin suites available for WordPress.

Can I manage a series with AuthorKit Books?

Yes. AuthorKit Books supports full series management with series name, book number, and custom taxonomies so readers can easily discover all books in a series.
