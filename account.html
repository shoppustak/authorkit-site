How to Activate Your License

  1. 1

    Download the plugin from your customer portal

  2. 2

    Install the plugin in WordPress (Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin)

  3. 3

    Activate the plugin in WordPress

  4. 4

    Go to Settings → AuthorKit → License

  5. 5

    Enter your license key and click "Activate License"

  6. 6

    You're all set! Premium features are now unlocked
